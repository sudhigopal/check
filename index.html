Say Yes to Networks

At the University of Oklahoma, Sridhar Radhakrishnan and his research students are focusing on the following:

  • Development of algorithms for processing extreme-scale networks on distributed systems and desktop parallel systems such as the GPGPUs.
  • Development of algorithms which are at the intersection of machine learning and network science.
  • Development of algorithms for compressing massive amounts of networked data.
  • Development of algorithms which are at the intersection of network science and financial engineering.

In the recent past we focused on the following:

  • Design of protocols for Software Defined Networks, including the development of an SDN docker, and Fog computing.
  • Design of algorithms and protocols for RFID systems.
  • Design of algorithms and protocols for multicasting.
